Documents required for international trip

  1. Passport

    • Must be valid for at least 6 months from the date of travel.
    • Ensure it has enough blank pages for visa stamps.
  2. Visa
    • Tourist, student, work, or transit visa, depending on your purpose of visit.
    • Some countries offer visa-on-arrival or e-visa options.

  3. Flight Tickets

    • Confirmed round-trip or onward tickets.

  4. Travel Insurance

    • Covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, or lost luggage.

  5. Accommodation Details

    • Hotel bookings or a letter of invitation from the host (if staying with someone).

  6. Proof of Funds

    • Bank statements, credit card limit, or traveler's cheques to prove financial solvency.

  7. ID Proof

    • Government-issued identification other than your passport (optional but recommended).

  8. Health Documentation

    • Vaccination certificates (e.g., COVID-19, yellow fever, if required by the destination).
    • Medical prescriptions for any essential medications.

  9. Travel Itinerary

    • Detailed plan of your trip, including destinations and activities.

  10. International Driver's License (if applicable)

    • Required if you plan to drive in the foreign country.

  11. Consent Letter for Minors

    • If traveling with children, a consent letter from parents/guardians may be needed.

  12. Customs Documents

    • If carrying valuable items or currency exceeding the permitted limit, declare them at customs.

Documents required for national trip

  1. Government-Issued ID

    • Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Driving License, Voter ID, or Passport.
    • Ensure the ID matches the name on your ticket and bookings.

  2. Travel Tickets

    • Confirmed train, bus, or flight tickets.

  3. Accommodation Details

    • Hotel booking confirmation or other accommodation proof (e.g., homestays).

  4. Health Documentation (if applicable)

    • Medical prescriptions for essential medicines.
    • Recent vaccination certificates if required by certain states (e.g., during pandemics).

  5. Vehicle Documents (if traveling by car)

    • Driving License.
    • Vehicle Registration Certificate (RC).
    • Insurance and Pollution Under Control (PUC) certificate.

  6. Student/Work ID (optional)

    • If availing discounts or for official travel.

  7. Consent Letter for Minors

    • If traveling with children without both parents, a consent letter may be required in certain situations.

  8. Itinerary

    • A basic plan of your trip, including destinations and emergency contacts.
