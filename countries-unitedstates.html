Welcome to the United States

Historical and Cultural Highlights

The United States is a vast and diverse nation, known for its innovation, cultural influence, and rich history. From the founding of the country in 1776 to becoming a global powerhouse, the U.S. has a story filled with triumphs and challenges.

The country’s journey spans the American Revolution, the Civil Rights Movement, and technological revolutions that have shaped the world. Its cultural influence is reflected in global music, movies, and technology. National landmarks such as the Lincoln Memorial and the Library of Congress tell stories of freedom, perseverance, and progress.
